# Transform Data
### Step 1: Transforming Timestamp Data into Month
- Go to the Home tab > click Transform Data

<img src="image\click Transform Data.png" width="100%" height="40%">

- Select the timestamp column > right-click > choose Duplicate Column

<img src="image\Select the Timestamp column , right-click and choose Duplicate Column.png" width="100%" height="40%">

- Go to the duplicated column (named timestamp - Copy) > right-click > Transform > Month > Name of Month

<img src="image\Transform, Month and Name of Month.png" width="100%" height="40%">
<img src="image\Name of Month.png" width="100%" height="40%">

- Double-click the column header to rename it to MonthName

<img src="image\Double-click the column header to rename.png" width="100%" height="40%">
<img src="image\MonthName.png" width="100%" height="40%">

- Click Close & Apply on the left side

<img src="image\Click Close & Apply.png" width="100%" height="40%">
