# Data Analyst Project: Audible Data Visualization in Power BI

## Project Overview
### Requirement Gathering

An author who sells audiobooks on the Audible platform would like to create a sales report for their books distributed through Audible.

The author wants to analyze the following information:

- Revenue Performance: Total revenue generated from audiobook sales on Audible since the launch date, and overall sales performance over time.

- Customer Countries: Identify which countries customers are purchasing from in order to plan the next fan meeting location.

- Top 5 Best-Selling Books: Determine the top 5 best-selling audiobooks to prioritize for additional promotion.

- Sales Trend: Analyze sales trends over time (monthly or yearly growth patterns).

- Additional Book Details: View detailed information about each book, such as title, author, genre, price, units sold, and other relevant metrics.
